03 — How it works

Signal in. Evidence out.

01

Ingest

Repos, images, cloud roles, host agents, gateways, and vendor documents — pulled into one normalized asset graph.

02

Reason

Deterministic rules do the provable work; an AI loop plans, chains, and correlates the parts that need judgement.

03

Score

Every finding gets calibrated confidence and cross-signal corroboration — no black-box verdicts, no hallucinated citations.

04

Prove

Explainable output: what, where, why, and how to fix — a report, a VEX decision, or an examiner-defensible trace.
